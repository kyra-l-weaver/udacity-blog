Staying Overnight

Free fun vacation destination! In nature! Need I say more?? There's one catch: Camping in Harriman is 100% primitive. What does that mean? Nothing, not even water, not even toilets, nothing! is provided. There are 9 lean-to's around the park, and staying in them overnight is on a first-come, first-serve basis. You may also pitch a tent within 150' of any of these shelters. Anywhere else is technically illegal, so don't risk it. Here is the list of shelters:
  • Dutch Doctor
  • Tom Jones
  • Bald Rocks
  • Big Hill
  • Fingerboard
Note: there are bears in the park. I have seen one personally. It was small and walked away as soon as it saw me, but I would not trust it to stay out of my tent if there were food in it. Make sure to bring a bear bag and know how to hang it. There are cables with pre-tied carabiners for you to mount your bags by most shelters.
